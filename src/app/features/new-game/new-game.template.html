LoopingAges

By MorpheusZero

You wake up, or at least you think you did? You don't really remember falling asleep in the first place. You get up from the ground and look around. There is nothing but wilderness. You reach in your pocket and find a note--"Is that MY handwriting?"--

"The time continuum is still broken. I need to gather more Paradox Tokens to fix the timeline or I'll be stuck repeating this loop forever...REMEMBER!"

The note trails off into more details about how I need to collect dark matter and time particles to advance through various ages of civilization to break free of this time loop. It doesn't make much sense, and I don't know who wrote this--I don't even remember my own name. Looks like we are starting in the Stone Age. I guess I am some sort of time traveler now then? I probably need a new name...
